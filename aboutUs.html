About Us


Why Pennington Ice Cream?

At Pennington Ice Cream, we believe that our commitment to quality, freshness, and community sets us apart from other ice cream suppliers. Here are just a few reasons why you should choose Pennington Ice Cream for all of your frozen treat needs:

  • Quality Ingredients:

    • We use only the freshest, locally sourced ingredients to make our ice cream. We believe that by using high-quality ingredients, we can create a product that is not only delicious but also healthier and better for the environment.

  • Handmade:

    • All of our ice cream is made by hand, in small batches, using traditional techniques. We believe that this approach allows us to create ice cream that is richer, creamier, and more flavorful than mass-produced ice cream.

  • Unique Flavors:

    • We offer a wide range of unique and creative flavors, from classic favorites like vanilla and chocolate to more adventurous options like lavender honey and bourbon pecan. We are constantly experimenting with new flavors and techniques to keep our customers coming back for more.

  • Community Support:

    • We are committed to supporting our local community by sourcing our ingredients from local farmers and suppliers. We believe that by supporting local businesses, we can create a stronger, more sustainable community.

  • Customer Service:

    • At Pennington Ice Cream, we value our customers above all else. We strive to provide friendly, personalized service and to create an experience that is both fun and memorable.

Thank you for buying Pennington Ice Cream. We are proud of the quality of our products and the commitment we have to our community. We look forward to serving you and your family for years to come.
